# INE5406 - Digital Systems

A course that deepens the Digital Circuits foundations, with hardware design in VHDL on an FPGA board. The group work was done with Victória Rodrigues.

The practical side is made of in-class activities and four graded assignments over the semester, ending in Assignment IV — a **convolution module (Gaussian filter)** implemented in VHDL, with Python scripts to generate and filter the test matrices.

- `Atividades em Aula/` — in-class exercises
- `Prática 1` to `Prática 4` — the graded assignments (VHDL + reports)
