# EEL5105 - Digital Circuits and Techniques

This course has both theory and hands-on lab classes. In the labs we learned to program in VHDL on the DE1-SoC FPGA board. The practical part is made of in-class activities, 4 graded exercises over the semester and a final project.

## Final Project
| Final Project | [Project Folder](./Trabalho%20Final%20da%20Disciplina) |
| ------ | :-----------------------: |

The final project is an FPGA memory game. The player picks a difficulty and starts; for a few seconds, numbers from 1 to 15 in hexadecimal are shown and must be memorized. Then the player has 10 seconds to set them on the board switches.
